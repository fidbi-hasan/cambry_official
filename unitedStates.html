Visa Requirements & Application Process

Understanding the steps for your F-1 Student Visa.

  • Step 1

    Obtain I-20

    Receive your Form I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility) from a SEVP-approved school.

  • Step 2

    Pay SEVIS Fee

    Pay the I-901 SEVIS fee online using your I-20 details.

  • Step 3

    Complete DS-160 Form

    Fill out the non-immigrant visa application (Form DS-160) online.

  • Step 4

    Pay Visa Fee & Schedule Interview

    Pay the visa application fee (MRV fee) and schedule your visa interview and biometrics appointment.

  • Step 5

    Attend Visa Interview

    Attend your F-1 visa interview at the US Embassy/Consulate with all required documents.
