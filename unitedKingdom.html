Study in United Kingdom from Bangladesh

The United Kingdom is a premier destination for Bangladeshi students, renowned for its academic excellence and globally respected qualifications. Home to some of the world's oldest and most prestigious universities, the UK offers a rich multicultural environment in cities like London, Manchester, Edinburgh, and Glasgow.

The UK's shorter course durations (1-year Master's, 3-year Bachelor's) make it a cost-effective choice. Popular fields include Business, Law, Engineering, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Health Sciences. The Graduate Route visa allows students to work for 2 years post-study.

Visa Requirements & Application Process

Understanding the steps for your UK Student Visa.

  • Step 1

    Obtain CAS

    Receive a Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) from your university.

  • Step 2

    Prepare Documents

    Gather proof of funds (28 days bank statement), TB test certificate, and English test scores (IELTS).

  • Step 3

    Apply Online & Pay Fees

    Complete the online application at the GOV.UK website and pay the visa fee (£490) & IHS fee.

  • Step 4

    Biometric Appointment

    Book and attend your biometrics (fingerprints and photo) appointment at a VFS Centre.

  • Step 5

    Receive Visa Decision

    Await the decision and receive your visa vignette (sticker) to travel to the UK.
