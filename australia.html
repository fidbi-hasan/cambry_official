Visa Requirements & Application Process

All students are choosy when it comes to selecting their study destination. Many students have preferred Australia.

  • Step 1

    Obtain Offer Letter

    Obtain an offer of admission from an Australian university.

  • Step 2

    Register OSHC

    Register for an OSHC (Overseas Student Health Cover) policy.

  • Step 3

    Submit Online Application

    Submit your online application through the Australian Immigration Website.

  • Step 4

    Provide Essential Documents

    Like passport, confirmation of enrollment, proof of financial capacity, and English test scores.

  • Step 5

    Attend Visa Interview

    Attend a visa interview (if required) and await the decision.
