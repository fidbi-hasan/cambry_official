Visa Requirements & Application Process

Understanding the steps for your Swedish Residence Permit for Studies.

  • Step 1

    Receive Offer & Pay Tuition

    Receive your admission notification and pay the first tuition fee installment to the university.

  • Step 2

    Prepare Documents

    Gather passport, proof of tuition payment, health insurance, and bank statements for living costs (SEK 10,314/month).

  • Step 3

    Submit Online Application

    Apply for a Residence Permit online via the Swedish Migration Agency (Migrationsverket) website.

  • Step 4

    Pay Application Fee

    Pay the application fee (approx. SEK 1,500) online and await a decision.

  • Step 5

    Provide Biometrics & Get Permit

    After approval, visit the Swedish Embassy/VFS in Dhaka to give biometrics and get your Residence Permit card.
