Study in Malaysia from Bangladesh

Malaysia is a top choice for Bangladeshi students, offering high-quality education at a very affordable cost. Visa Requirements & Application Process

Understanding the steps to get your Malaysian Student Pass.

  • Step 1

    Obtain Offer Letter

    Receive an unconditional offer of admission from an accredited Malaysian institution.

  • Step 2

    Apply for Visa Approval Letter (VAL)

    Submit your application through the Education Malaysia Global Services (EMGS) portal.

  • Step 3

    Receive eVAL & Single Entry Visa (SEV)

    Once VAL is approved, download the eVAL. Apply for a Single Entry Visa (SEV) at a Malaysian embassy (if required).

  • Step 4

    Arrive in Malaysia & Medical Screening

    Travel to Malaysia with your eVAL and SEV. You must undergo a medical screening within 7 days of arrival.

  • Step 5

    Receive Student Pass Endorsement

    Submit your passport to your university for the final Student Pass sticker endorsement by the Immigration Department.
