Visa Requirements & Application Process

All students are choosy when it comes to selecting their study destination. Many students have preferred New Zealand.

  • Step 1

    Obtain Offer of Place

    Obtain an 'Offer of Place' from an approved New Zealand education provider.

  • Step 2

    Gather Documents

    Prepare key documents: Offer of Place, proof of identity, financial evidence, and health/character certificates.

  • Step 3

    Submit Online Application

    Apply for your 'Fee Paying Student Visa' online via the Immigration New Zealand website.

  • Step 4

    Pay Fee & Await AIP

    Pay the visa application fee and upload all required documents. Await your 'Approval in Principle' (AIP).

  • Step 5

    Pay Tuition & Receive e-Visa

    Once you get the AIP, pay your tuition fees. Submit the receipt to receive your final e-Visa.
