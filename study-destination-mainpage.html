Requirements to Study Abroad

If you are a student from Bangladesh planning to study in Australia, there are certain requirements you need to fulfil in order to pursue your education in this vibrant country. Here are some key points to consider:

Academic Qualification

You must meet the specific academic entry requirements for your chosen program and provide proof of completing your secondary education.

English Language Proficiency

International students must demonstrate English proficiency through tests like IELTS, TOEFL, or PTE, meeting the minimum score required by the university.

Financial Capacity

You must show sufficient funds to cover tuition, living expenses, and other study-related costs during your stay in Australia.

Overseas Student Health Cover

All international students must purchase OSHC to access healthcare services while studying in Australia.

Student Visa

You need to apply for the Student Visa (Subclass 500) with a Confirmation of Enrollment (CoE) and meet health, character, and GTE requirements.

Student Visa Cost

Bangladeshi applicants must pay the applicable visa application fee, which varies by course duration and visa subclass.
