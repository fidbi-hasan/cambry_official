Study in Canada from Bangladesh

Canada is a global leader in education, attracting students worldwide with its top-ranking universities, high academic standards, and focus on research. For Bangladeshi students, it offers a safe, tolerant, and multicultural environment. Cities like Toronto, Vancouver, Montreal, and Ottawa are major hubs for international students. The Canadian education system emphasizes practical skills and co-operative (co-op) education, providing valuable work experience. Popular fields include Computer Science, Engineering, Business, Health Sciences, and Data Science. Graduates are also eligible for the Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP).

Visa Requirements & Application Process

Understanding the steps for your Canadian Study Permit.

  • Step 1

    Obtain Letter of Acceptance (LOA)

    Receive an official LOA from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI).

  • Step 2

    Arrange Finances (GIC & Tuition)

    Purchase a Guaranteed Investment Certificate (GIC) of CAD 20,635 and pay the first year's tuition fees.

  • Step 3

    Submit Online Application (SDS)

    Apply online for a Study Permit via the IRCC portal (Student Direct Stream - SDS is recommended).

  • Step 4

    Give Biometrics & Medicals

    Receive a Biometric Instruction Letter (BIL) and complete your biometrics. Undergo an upfront medical examination.

  • Step 5

    Receive Passport Request (PPR)

    Upon approval, receive a Passport Request Letter. Submit your passport to the VFS for visa stamping.
